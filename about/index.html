Postgraduate Exam Study Schedule (Strict Mode)

Prove your discipline deserves a graduate seat!

Time Task
7:20 AM Wake up, dress, freshen up, skincare, breakfast, arrive at study room
8:00 - 11:30 AM Math (3.5h)
11:30 AM - 1:00 PM Lunch + nap (1.5h)
1:00 - 5:00 PM Computer Science (408) (4h)
5:00 - 6:00 PM Exercise
6:00 - 7:00 PM Dinner
7:00 - 10:00 PM Math + English (3h)
10:00 - 10:30 PM Review & Recap (0.5h)
10:30 - 11:00 PM Wash up + laundry
11:30 PM Lights out — Zero exceptions!

Rules

  • No overtime studying: Strict 11:00 PM bedtime.
  • Buffer time: 1.5h/day for chores/breaks (e.g., laundry, skincare).
  • Short breaks: 10-15min per 2h study block (stay focused!).
  • Weekly reward: After 3 full study days, take a 2h break (movie/walk).

Daily Structure: Your waking hours are divided into three main study blocks:

Morning (3.5h): 8:00 AM – 11:30 AM

Afternoon (4h): 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM

Evening (3h): 7:00 PM – 10:00 PM

Rest & Recovery:

7.5h sleep (11:00 PM – 6:50 AM) – No sleep debt!

1.5h buffer time daily for chores (laundry, skincare, errands), extra study, or short breaks.

Break Strategy: Within each 4h study block, take short breaks (e.g., 2x 15min or 3x 10min). Stay disciplined—no excessive slacking!

Weekly recharge: Take 1-2 full study blocks off per week OR, after 3 straight study days, reward yourself with an extra 2h break (e.g., movie, walk). With proper sleep, you won’t need “catch-up” naps.

Stay sharp, stay consistent—your effort determines your success!

我的考研作息时间(必须遵守版) 看看你自己的努力程度配不配的上研究生!!!!!!!

时间 任务
7:20 起床、穿衣、洗漱、护肤、早饭、到自习室
8:00-11:30 数学(3h30min)
11:30-13:00 午饭、午休(1.5h)
13:00-17:00 408(4h)
17:00-18:00 锻炼
18:00:19:00 吃饭
19:00-22:00 数学+英语(3h)
22:00-22:30 复盘(0.5h)
22:30-23:00 洗漱+洗衣服
23:30 熄灯睡觉，绝不熬夜

一天清醒的时间分三大段：上午3h30min，下午4h，晚上4h ①上午4h：早上7:30-11:30（早上8:00-12:00）

午饭+午休=2h

②下午4h：下午1:30-5:30（下午2:00-6:00）

晚饭+晚休=1h

③晚上4h：晚上7-11点（晚上6:30-10:30）

睡觉7.5h。早睡早起，23:20睡觉，6:50起床 每天还余出来1.5h做点杂务：拿拿快递，洗洗衣服，护肤，锻炼，或者加班学习，或者发呆。

每天三个大段的4h也不可能完全专注不休息，每个大段可以累计小休30分钟，3次10分钟或者2次15分钟的课间。毕竟大段与大段之间都是个把小时的休息，大段之内不要休息太多了，不然太摸了，你的努力配得上研究生嘛。

关于每周或半周的大休，可以选择每周休息1-2个大段，或者每连学三天, 在本应该学习的时间额外再休息半个大段(2h)，看个电影或者溜溜放松一下。平常每天早睡早起7.5小时睡眠，你也不需要补觉。
